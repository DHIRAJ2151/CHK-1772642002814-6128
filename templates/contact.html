Frequently Asked Questions

How can I place an order?

You can place orders through our website, by calling our customer service, or by visiting our physical store. We accept various payment methods including online banking, credit cards, and cash on delivery.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for most products. Agricultural equipment and chemicals have specific return conditions. Please contact our customer service for detailed information about returns and refunds.

Do you provide delivery services?

Yes, we provide delivery services across India. Delivery charges and timeframes vary based on location and product type. Free delivery is available for orders above ₹5,000 in select areas.

How accurate is your disease detection system?

Our AI-powered disease detection system has an accuracy rate of over 90% for common plant diseases. However, we always recommend consulting with agricultural experts for critical decisions.
