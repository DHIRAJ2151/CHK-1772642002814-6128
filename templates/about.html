About Krushi

Empowering farmers with modern technology and traditional wisdom since 2025

Our Story

Krushi was founded in 2025 by a group of agricultural experts and technology enthusiasts who recognized the need to bridge the gap between traditional farming practices and modern technological solutions.

What started as a small local initiative has grown into a comprehensive agricultural solutions platform, serving thousands of farmers across the country. Our journey has been driven by a simple yet powerful mission: to make farming more profitable, sustainable, and accessible.

Today, Krushi stands as a trusted partner for farmers, providing everything from high-quality equipment and fertilizers to expert advice and cutting-edge disease detection technology.

Our Mission

To empower farmers with innovative solutions that increase productivity, reduce costs, and promote sustainable agricultural practices. We believe that every farmer deserves access to the tools and knowledge needed to succeed.

Our Vision

To become the leading agricultural solutions provider in India, driving the transformation of farming from traditional methods to smart, technology-driven agriculture that ensures food security and farmer prosperity.
